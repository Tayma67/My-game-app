AI Resume Prompts: Tailor Your Resume and Beat the ATS in 20 Minutes

Updated 2026 · ~5 min read

Generic applications get ignored, and tailoring each one by hand takes forever. A free ChatGPT or Claude account fixes both — if you use the right prompts. Here's a fast, honest workflow that keeps your resume truthful while matching each job.

1. Check what your resume is missing (ATS gap)

Compare my resume against this job description and list: (a) important keywords/skills MISSING from my resume, (b) where I could naturally add each, (c) any I should NOT add because I lack the skill. Resume: "[PASTE]". Job: "[PASTE]".

2. Tailor your bullets to the role

Rewrite my resume bullet points to match this role: mirror the job's keywords, lead each bullet with a strong action verb, and quantify impact. Keep everything truthful — do not invent experience. Resume: "[PASTE]". Job: "[PASTE]".

3. Write a cover letter that isn't generic

Write a cover letter for [ROLE] at [COMPANY] using my background "[PASTE]" and this job "[PASTE]". Three short paragraphs: why this company, two relevant proofs, a confident close. No "to whom it may concern".

4. Prep interview answers with STAR

Help me answer "[QUESTION]" using STAR. My experience: "[PASTE]". Keep it under 90 seconds spoken, end on a quantified result, and flag anything that sounds rehearsed.
