## 1. Tailor your resume to a specific job
> Act as a professional resume writer. Here is my current resume: "[PASTE]".
> Here is the job description: "[PASTE]". Rewrite my resume bullet points to
> match this role: mirror the job's keywords, lead each bullet with a strong
> action verb, quantify impact. Keep everything truthful — do not invent
> experience.

## 2. Beat the ATS (keyword check)
> Compare my resume against this job description and list: (a) important
> keywords/skills MISSING from my resume, (b) where I could naturally add
> each, (c) any I should NOT add because I lack the skill. Resume: "[PASTE]".
> Job: "[PASTE]".

## 3. Build a STAR interview answer
> Help me answer "[QUESTION]" using STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
> My raw experience: "[PASTE]". Keep it ≤90 seconds spoken, end on a
> quantified result, flag anything that sounds rehearsed.
