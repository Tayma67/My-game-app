# The Small Business Social Media AI Kit — A Month of Content in an Hour

12 copy-paste AI prompts to plan, write, and repurpose a month of social media
content for your business — on a free ChatGPT or Claude account. No agency, no
guesswork, no staring at a blank caption box.

> How to use: replace [BRACKETS] with your business details. Edit outputs to
> sound like you. Post consistently — the prompts make content; consistency
> makes results.

---

## Part 1 — Plan

## 1. Build a 30-day content calendar
> Act as a social media strategist. My business: "[WHAT YOU DO + audience]".
> Build a 30-day content calendar with a mix of: educational tips, behind-the-
> scenes, customer proof, promotions (max 20%), and engagement questions. For
> each day give a content type + a one-line idea. Keep it realistic for a
> solo owner.

## 2. Find your content pillars
> Based on my business "[DESCRIBE]", suggest 4-5 content pillars (recurring
> themes) I can rotate through so I never run out of ideas. For each, give 3
> example post ideas.

---

## Part 2 — Write

## 3. Batch a week of captions
> Write 7 social captions for "[BUSINESS]". Mix: 3 value/tips, 2 behind-the-
> scenes, 1 soft promo, 1 question. Each ≤280 chars, distinct hook in line one,
> no hashtag spam. Number them Mon–Sun.

## 4. The scroll-stopping hook
> Give me 10 first-line hooks for a post about "[TOPIC]" for "[AUDIENCE]".
> Make them curiosity- or problem-driven, no clickbait that I can't deliver on.

## 5. Promotional post that doesn't feel salesy
> Write a promo post for "[OFFER]". Lead with the customer's problem, show the
> outcome, make the offer clear, one CTA. Friendly, not pushy. ≤200 chars.

## 6. Reusable hashtag sets
> Give me 3 hashtag sets (10 each) for "[NICHE]": one broad-reach, one niche,
> one local/community. Avoid banned or spammy tags.

---

## Part 3 — Repurpose

## 7. One idea → five platforms
> Turn this idea into a content pack: "[PASTE idea/blog/transcript]". Output a
> LinkedIn post, an Instagram caption, 3 tweets/X posts, a short-video script
> (≤45s), and an email snippet. Keep my voice consistent.

## 8. Turn a review into content
> Turn this customer review into a shareable social post (with permission):
> "[PASTE review]". Highlight the result, add a warm thank-you, invite others.
> Keep it authentic, not braggy.

## 9. Short-video (Reels/TikTok) script
> Write a 30-45s vertical video script for "[TOPIC]": a 3-second hook, 3 quick
> points, and a CTA. Include on-screen text suggestions. Faceless-friendly.

---

## Part 4 — Engage & Measure

## 10. Reply to comments and DMs faster
> Write friendly reply templates for common situations: a compliment, a price
> question, a complaint, and a collaboration request. On-brand for "[BUSINESS]",
> each ≤40 words.

## 11. Engagement questions that get comments
> Give me 12 simple questions to ask my audience "[NICHE]" that spark easy
> replies (this-or-that, fill-in-the-blank, quick opinions).

## 12. Monthly review prompt
> Here are my numbers/notes for the month: "[PASTE]". Tell me what worked, what
> to drop, 3 things to do more of next month, and 5 fresh post ideas based on
> what performed.

---

→ schedule everything. A month of content, planned in about an hour.
