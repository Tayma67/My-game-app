# The Coach & Consultant's AI Kit — Sign Clients & Deliver Faster

12 copy-paste AI prompts to attract clients, run discovery calls, write
proposals, and deliver coaching/consulting work — on a free ChatGPT or Claude
account. No fancy funnels, no expensive software.

> How to use: replace [BRACKETS]. AI drafts; your expertise and judgment make
> it valuable. Never present AI output as a substitute for real client care.

---

## Part 1 — Attract

## 1. Clarify your offer in one sentence
> Act as a positioning expert. I help "[WHO]" achieve "[OUTCOME]" through
> "[METHOD]". Write 5 one-sentence positioning statements that are specific,
> outcome-focused, and free of jargon.

## 2. Content that attracts your ideal client
> Give me 10 short-post ideas for "[NICHE]" coaching that speak to my ideal
> client's pains and desires. For each: a hook line + the core point. No
> generic motivational fluff.

## 3. Lead magnet outline
> Outline a simple, valuable free resource (checklist or mini-guide) that my
> ideal client "[DESCRIBE]" would happily trade their email for. Give a title,
> 5-7 sections, and the single transformation it promises.

---

## Part 2 — Convert

## 4. Discovery-call question guide
> Write a discovery-call framework for "[SERVICE]": opening, 8 questions that
> uncover the prospect's real problem and stakes, a transition to my offer,
> and a soft close. Consultative, not pushy.

## 5. Proposal that closes
> Write a coaching/consulting proposal for [CLIENT] who wants "[OUTCOME]".
> Sections: their situation, the goal, my approach (phases), what's included,
> investment options, and next step. Confident, outcome-led.

## 6. Handle the "I need to think about it"
> The prospect says they need to think about it: "[CONTEXT]". Write a
> respectful reply that surfaces the real objection (price, timing, trust) and
> offers a small next step, without pressure.

## 7. Pricing tiers
> Help me design 3 pricing tiers for "[SERVICE]": a starter, a core, and a
> premium. For each: who it's for, what's included, and the transformation.
> Anchor the premium to make the core the obvious choice.

---

## Part 3 — Deliver

## 8. Session plan
> Create a structured plan for a [60-minute] session on "[TOPIC]" for a client
> at "[STAGE]": objective, opening, core exercise, key questions, and a
> homework/action assignment.

## 9. Turn a session into a recap + action plan
> Here are my session notes: "[PASTE]". Write a warm client recap: what we
> covered, key insights, agreed actions (Owner — action — by when), and
> encouragement. Keep my voice.

## 10. Reframe a stuck client (coaching questions)
> My client is stuck on "[SITUATION]". Give me 8 powerful coaching questions
> (not advice) that help them see it differently and find their own next step.

---

## Part 4 — Retain & Grow

## 11. Check-in + accountability message
> Write a brief mid-program check-in message for a client working toward
> "[GOAL]": acknowledge progress, gently hold accountability, ask one focusing
> question. Supportive, not nagging.

## 12. Referral & testimonial request
> Write a natural message asking a happy client for a testimonial and a
> referral. Make it specific to their result "[RESULT]", low-pressure, and
> easy to say yes to.
